about me

As a musical theatre performer I have shared the stage with Ben Vereen, Rebecca Caine, Colm Wilkinson and Andrew Lloyd Webber. I have performed at the Victoria Playhouse Petrolia for the past four seasons and have also played leading roles in a wide variety of musicals including RENT, Beauty And The Beast, The Wizard of Oz and number of Canadian premiers as well as the Dora Award nominated original musical review Off-Broadway, On Stage. I was also a finalist in the CBC hit show How Do You Solve A Problem Like Maria? I have earned critical acclaim for my portrayal of Liza Minnelli in my one woman show Liza Live! as well as for my rock cabaret Jagged Little Me, an autobiographical journey through Alanis Morissettes iconic 1996 album. I have also produced a number of solo shows and cabarets as well as several sold out events for both the Toronto Pride and Toronto Fringe Festivals.

I am also a curator of the Sunday Cabaret Series at Toronto's 120 Diner, working with and mentoring young performers in the art of Cabaret. I am the host/producer of SINGular Sensation, Toronto's premier weekly musical theatre open stage that recently celebrated their sixth year in the city. I have also hosted events for Second City Toronto, The Musical Stage Company and Theatre Passe Murielle. Most recently I was featured on the cover of NOW Magazine, Toronto's largest news magazine and named and "artist to watch" by the Globe & Mail for my roles in four Toronto Fringe productions as well as my contributions to theatre and cabaret in Toronto. As a voice actor I have voiced recurring character on a number of animated series' including PBS' Peg+ Cat and Amazon's Creative Galaxy and I am currently the voice of Family Jr. When not on stage or behind the mic, I am a voice teacher, dance and voice over to young people.
